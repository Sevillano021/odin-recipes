How to Make Açai Bowl, the Healthiest Breakfast or Snack

The açai bowl is one of the best ways to start the day. The healthiest and best breakfast for when you need a powerful energy boost to get through the morning. It's easy to prepare, similar to a smoothie bowl: just blend milk, powdered açai, and frozen fruit, and top with cereals, seeds, nuts, or whatever you like best.

Açai is considered a superfood: it's antioxidant, rich in calcium, protein, fiber, and much more. It helps keep the immune system strong and prevents cardiovascular diseases. These berries, which look a lot like blueberries, are grown in Brazil and arrive in Spain dehydrated and powdered. With them, we've prepared our açai bowl. You can watch the video and subscribe to our YouTube channel or follow the traditional step-by-step below.

Ingredients

Serves 2
  • Banana (frozen) 2
  • Blueberries (frozen) 40
  • Raspberries (frozen) 25
  • Almonds 6
  • Milk 150 ml
  • Ground açai 5 g
  • Poppy seeds
  • Oat flakes
  • Apples

How to Make Açai Bowl

Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time 10 m
  • Preparation 10 m

To make the açai bowl, the fruit must be frozen so the consistency is creamy. Before preparing, peel and slice the bananas and freeze them. Make sure the blueberries and raspberries are also frozen.

Place the frozen fruit in a powerful blender along with the almonds. Add half of the milk and the ground açai (five grams is about one level tablespoon). Blend until smooth and check the thickness. Add the rest of the milk little by little, blending again until you reach the desired consistency.

It is very important to have a powerful blender because the frozen fruit pieces are tough. Any American-style blender works well. You can also make the açai bowl with a Thermomix, which is the appliance I use at home. I gradually increased the speed from five to seven for about 30 seconds.

Serve the açai bowl in a bowl and decorate with poppy seeds, oat flakes, chopped apples, and banana slices. You can also add more berries if you like.
