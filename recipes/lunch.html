Homemade Beef Meatballs in Vegetable and Sherry Sauce

Homemade beef meatballs in vegetable and sherry sauce are a classic of Spanish cuisine, perfect for a comforting lunch. This recipe combines juicy beef with a rich vegetable sauce and the special touch of sherry wine.

Ingredients

Serves 4
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour (for coating)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 ripe tomato
  • 100 ml sherry wine
  • 300 ml beef stock
Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time: 50 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

How to Make Homemade Beef Meatballs in Vegetable and Sherry Sauce

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground beef with the egg, chopped garlic, breadcrumbs, milk, parsley, salt, and pepper. Shape the meatballs and coat them in flour.
  2. Fry the meatballs in a pan with olive oil until golden on the outside. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© the chopped onion, carrot, and green pepper until soft. Add the peeled and chopped tomato and cook a few minutes more.
  4. Add the sherry wine and let the alcohol evaporate. Add the beef stock and blend the sauce if desired.
  5. Return the meatballs to the pan with the sauce and cook over low heat for 20-25 minutes, until tender.
  6. Serve hot, accompanied by white rice, potatoes, or bread.
